Our Mission

  • To achieve an equitable and sustainable society, target every sustainability sphere: environmental, social, and economical.
  • To spread awareness on environmental degradation, climate change, sustainable development, and holistic living among the common masses.
  • To promote the practice of Recycle, Reuse, and Reduce.
  • To engage the farmers on organic and climate-resilient farming; arrange training programs and interactive sessions with experts to inform them about the adverse effects of using chemical fertilizers and pesticides in the agricultural land.
  • To protect and preserve diverse natural resources (e.g., biodiversity).
  • To advocate the rights of equal distribution of natural resources among all by sensitizing the common people.
  • To promote education for sustainable living in schools: encouraging them to implement organic kitchen garden, waste management, and wastewater treatment.
  • To bridge the gap between research and practical implementation by actively collaborating with the research groups for sustainable technological inventions.
  • To involve in policy-making for the benefit of the environment and human beings.
  • To implement water management, water recycling, rainwater harvesting
  • To grow self-consciousness on equality in every aspect among the common masses.
  • To establish gender equality empowers women and other marginalized/ deprived communities by making them self-independent through cottage industry, handicrafts, and various sustainable enterprises.
  • To organise/ assist other organisations by providing relief to the people affected by flood, earthquake, and other natural calamities or any disaster/crises.
