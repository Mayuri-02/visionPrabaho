About Our Story

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others” – Mahatma Gandhi


Inspired by this ethos, we engage towards achieving an equitable and sustainable. The irreversible effects of climate change pose a significant threat to all of us due to our uncontrolled exploitation and human-induced destruction of natural resources.


Environmental degradation-based causes are responsible for 24% of the global deaths, which includes 28% of mortality for children under the age of five. Thus, immediate action from all the stakeholders is required to restore the human well-being ecosystem. We, Vision Prabaho, are working to attain an equitable and sustainable society, targeting each aspect of sustainability: i.e., environmental, social, and economical. However, a common misconception arises every time we talk about sustainability: economic progress and environmental protection can not go hand in hand. In reality, a tread-off must be maintained between the three aspects of sustainability. Our goals towards the synergy include embracing education, empowerment, and equality to make our "ONLY HOME" healthier and adaptively resilient.
